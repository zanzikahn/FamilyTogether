## Can I Get Points Back?

**No.** Once you claim a reward, the points are gone.

If you change your mind after clicking, you cannot get a refund.

{% hint style="warning" %}
**Think Before You Click!**

Ask yourself:
- Do I really want this?
- Is there something better I should save for?
- Can I earn more points soon?
{% endhint %}
